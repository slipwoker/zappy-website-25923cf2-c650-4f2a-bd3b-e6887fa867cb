Our Story

Craft, intention, and theart of visual thinking

Lior Design began as a quiet conviction: that thoughtful visual work changes how people feel about the things they use, choose, and trust. What started as a freelance practice has grown into a brand design studio built on deep listening, careful craft, and a genuine love of the design process.

Every project starts with questions rather than answers. Who is this for? What does it need to say without words? What makes this brand irreplaceable? We believe the most durable visual systems are born from honest conversations about purpose — not from templates or trend-chasing.

Our approach is deliberately unhurried. We move through research, concept, and refinement at a pace that lets ideas breathe. Sketches fill notebooks before pixels appear on screen. Print samples get handled, folded, and held up to light. That tactile loop between thinking and making is where the best work lives.

Thoughtful visual systems matter because they compound over time. A well-considered identity doesn't just look good at launch — it grows with the business, adapts across touchpoints, and builds the kind of recognition that no single campaign can manufacture. We design for longevity, not novelty.

Lior Design works with a small number of clients at a time, by choice. That intimacy lets us bring full attention to every decision — from the weight of a typeface to the silence between elements on a page.

Our Philosophy

Design principles thatguide every decision

At Lior Design, a brand design studio rooted in intention, these values shape how we think, collaborate, and create — from the first brief to the final file.

Clarity

Every visual decision should communicate, not decorate. We strip away the superfluous until only the essential remains — a mark, a palette, a layout that speaks without noise.

Craft

Good design is made with care. We attend to kerning, weight, proportion, and color with the patience of someone who believes the details are the work — not an afterthought.

Warmth

Strategy and human connection are not opposites. We bring genuine curiosity to every client relationship, making the process feel collaborative, open, and grounded in trust.

Longevity

Trend-chasing ages fast. We design for the long view — identities and systems built to grow with a brand over years, not just look good in this week's portfolio.

Collaboration

The best outcomes emerge from dialogue. We work alongside our clients as creative partners, listening deeply and building on their knowledge to arrive somewhere neither of us could reach alone.

Strategic Restraint

Knowing what to leave out is a skill. We resist the impulse to fill every space, trusting that silence in design — whitespace, pause, simplicity — carries as much meaning as the marks we make.
