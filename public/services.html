How We Work

A process built on
clarity and craft

Every project at our brand design studio follows a rhythm that keeps things focused, collaborative, and genuinely enjoyable — from first conversation to final files.

01 Discovery

Understanding what you're really building

We begin with a deep listening session — part strategy conversation, part creative archaeology. We ask about your audience, your ambitions, and the feeling you want your brand to leave behind. This stage shapes everything that follows, so we take our time here and document what we learn together.

  • Brand intake questionnaire
  • Competitor and context audit
  • Kick-off conversation (60–90 min)
01
02
02 Direction

Setting a creative course before committing to details

Before a single logo is drawn, we present a visual direction — a curated mood board and strategic rationale that defines the tone, palette territory, and typographic character of your brand. You choose the direction that resonates. This alignment point saves weeks of revision later.

  • Two distinct creative directions
  • Visual mood and reference boards
  • One round of directional feedback
03 Refinement

Sharpening every detail until it's exactly right

With direction confirmed, we move into full design development. Logos, typography systems, colour palettes, and supporting elements are built with care and tested across contexts. Feedback rounds are structured and purposeful — we guide you through what to look for so revisions stay focused and productive.

  • Full identity system design
  • Two structured revision rounds
  • Real-world mockup presentations

04 Rollout

Handing over a brand you can actually use

Final delivery goes beyond file handoff. You receive a complete brand guidelines document, organised asset folders, and a walkthrough session so you know exactly how to apply your new identity. We remain available for questions after launch — because a great brand deserves a confident debut.

  • Brand guidelines document
  • Organised, print- and web-ready files
  • Post-launch support window
